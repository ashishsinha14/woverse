Skin type can be influenced by age. People typically experience changes in their skin as they age, and these changes can impact their skin type.

Dry

Dry Skin

Dry skin feels dehydrated, flaky, and more prone to irritation

Oily

Oily Skin

The imbalance between testosterone and oestrogen can manifest as oily skin and cause acne, with puss-filled spots and areas of the skin that are painful to touch. As a result, women who have never experienced acne might suddenly develop acne in their 30s, 40s or 50s

Combination

Combination

Combination skin is characterized by having different skin types in different areas of the face. The most common combination is a mix of oily and dry or normal areas.

Normal

Normal Skin

Normal skin has a balanced level of sebum production. It's neither excessively oily nor overly dry.

Perimenopause

Perimenopause

Perimenopause means "around menopause" and refers to the time during which your body makes the natural transition to menopause, It usually last 4-7 years and can start as early as mid 30's in Indian Women.

Postmenopause

Postmenopause

Postmenopause is the time after menopause, when a woman hasn't experienced a period for over a year.

Not Sure
