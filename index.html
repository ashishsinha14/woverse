What is WOVERSE coming up with?

Our first product range will start from the skincare section and include topical solutions to ease out peri-post menopausal symptoms. We are committed to offering non-toxic, cruelty-free, and eco-friendly products.

We believe in providing clean beauty options to women, ensuring transparency about the ingredients used in our products. With a focus on sustainability, our products incorporate environmentally friendly ingredients to preserve the earth.

We are dedicated to addressing the skincare needs of women in their late 30s and over 40s, who are often underserved in the beauty industry. Our goal is to offer a comprehensive range of products that target menopausal skincare concerns specific to this demographic.

Additionally, we understand the discomfort caused by symptoms such as hot flashes and vaginal dryness during the peri-post menopausal phase. To facilitate a smoother transition for every woman, we provide topical plant-based products designed to offer relief from these symptoms.

BUILDING COMMUNITY

We strongly believe that menopause is a transformative journey that deserves compassion, care, and a sense of community. Many women experiencing perimenopause or menopause often find themselves filled with confusion and numerous questions. They seek a supportive community where they can freely share their experiences and receive support from other women who can relate to their journey, in addition to seeking guidance from healthcare professionals. That's why we are dedicated to building a community of like-minded women who are eager to openly discuss menopause and provide support to one another. By fostering this community, we aim to create a safe space where women can connect, share their stories, find solace, and gain valuable insights from those who understand the unique challenges of menopause. Together, we can navigate this transformative phase with empathy and strength, empowering each other along the way.

AWARENESS

Creating awareness about menopause is crucial for our society. Let's Talk Menopause is dedicated to initiating a cultural movement. Menopause has long been veiled in secrecy, but it's time to change that. Our goal is to ignite conversations about menopause, encouraging open dialogue and understanding. We believe that when women no longer feel isolated, their menopause experience improves significantly. To achieve this, we are launching a multilingual public awareness campaign. By prominently featuring menopause on posters in transit stations and doctors' offices, as well as sharing information through social media, we aim to educate the public about menopause and emphasize the importance of being informed. Together, we can break the silence surrounding menopause and ensure that everyone has access to accurate knowledge about this significant life transition.
