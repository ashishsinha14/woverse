About Us

We asked ourselves, ‘Where do women in their 40s and 50s go to find solutions as they enter menopause?’

We believe all women deserve modern, innovative and chemical free products, necessary information that helps and supports them in there midlife journey with compassion, care and community.

Symptoms like hot flashes, jowls and sagging skin, fine lines, vaginal dryness, low libido, and bladder leaks should not stop any woman. We’re here to help you tap into your fierceness, your sexiness, your realness, your agelessness.

Women at this stage of life are the coolest, smartest, most interesting women we know. They deserve a place. They deserve to age gracefully and look beautiful forever.

Our Mission

To provide eco-friendly, cruelty-free, non toxic wellness products for overall body of women who are in peri- menopausal, menopausal or post-menopause stage so that they can age gracefully. They can carry their self as gracefully in the further journey of life as they have carried their families.

What WOVERSE is coming up with?

Our first product range will start from skin care section and topical solution to ease out the peri-post menopausal symptoms. Our products will be non-toxic, cruelty free and eco- friendly.

We want to give clean beauty products to women. Our product will be transparent to customer about its ingredient. Our products will use sustainable ingredients ensures that the earth is preserved.

We want to specifically address a suite of menopausal skincare issues for women in the late 30’s and over-40 set, an underserved group in the beauty industry.

We also want to address the symptoms like hot flashes, vaginal dryness in peri-post menopausal phase by giving topical plant-based products to relief the symptoms and make this transition journey smooth for every woman.

Solutions

BUILDING COMMUNITY

We believe menopause is a supportive journey and not dismissed, but met with compassion, care and community. Most of the women who are in perimenopause or menopause phase are going through confusion and lots of questions in their mind. They need a community where they can share their experiences and get support from other women rather than a doctor. We are building a community of likeminded women who want to talk openly about menopause and support each other.

RESEARCH BASED NATURAL PRODUCTS

We believe natural and ecofriendly way to solve any problem. We are coming with plant-based product to relieve menopausal symptoms. These products will be researched based and scientifically backed and will be safe to use.

INTELLIGENT MONITORING

We are building an app which will make your menopause journey easy. This app will log your symptoms and get personalized insights, will give you self-guided programs created by experts for your symptoms and give you community of women and experts for your support.

AWARENESS

This is the most needed part of our society to spread awareness about menopause. Let’s Talk Menopause is on a mission to culturally kick-start a movement. For too long, menopause has been a shrouded subject. We want to spark a conversation about menopause, so people understand it and talk openly about it. The less alone women feel, the better their menopause experience will be. That’s why we’re launching a multilingual public awareness campaign. By putting the topic out there physically—on posters in transit stations and doctors’ offices—and sharing it on social media, we aim to educate the public about what menopause is and why it’s essential that we all know the facts.
